Common questions

Health coverage FAQ

Do I pay anything for your help?

No. Jade Benefits is paid by the carrier when you enroll, not by you. Consultations, plan comparisons, and ongoing support are free. Your premium is the same whether you work with us or enroll on your own.

Can a 10 to 99 person business get a group plan?

Yes. Group coverage is our sweet spot. We build tailored group plans for teams of all sizes, handle the implementation, and stay on through renewals.

Do you cover truckers and owner operators?

Absolutely. Truckers need coverage that travels, so we focus on nationwide PPO networks that work wherever you haul. Read our dedicated guide on the blog.

I am self-employed or 1099. Do I qualify?

Yes. Freelancers, contractors, and solo owners can get strong individual and family options, and many qualify for premium subsidies. We review every credit you are eligible for.

Where are you located?

Our headquarters is in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and we help clients with coverage that reaches nationwide.
