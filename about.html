About us

Benefits built for the people who build things

Jade Benefits is a health insurance brokerage headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. We help small groups, small business owners, self-employed individuals, families, and truckers find coverage that fits.

Coverage without the runaround

Big carriers are built for big companies. The crews, drivers, and solo owners who keep the economy moving too often get left with confusing options and no one to call. We started Jade Benefits to change that.

We work with top A rated carriers and shop the whole market on your behalf. That means competitive premiums, low deductible options, PPO plans, and nationwide coverage, paired with a dedicated rep who knows your account by name.

Our consultations are free, our support is year round, and our advice is built around what actually fits your team or family, not whoever pays the highest commission.
