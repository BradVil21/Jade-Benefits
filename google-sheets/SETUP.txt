CONNECT YOUR WEBSITE FORMS TO GOOGLE SHEETS
===========================================

This sends every "Get a Quote" and "Contact Us" submission straight
into a Google Sheet you own. It is free and needs no server. Takes
about 5 minutes. Do it once.


STEP 1  -  Create the Google Sheet
----------------------------------
1. Go to https://sheets.google.com and create a new blank spreadsheet.
2. Name it something like "Jade Benefits Leads".
   (You do not need to add any tabs or headers. The script makes them.)


STEP 2  -  Add the script
-------------------------
1. In that spreadsheet, click  Extensions  >  Apps Script.
2. Delete whatever code is in the editor.
3. Open the file  Code.gs  (in this google-sheets folder), copy ALL of
   it, and paste it into the Apps Script editor.
4. Click the floppy disk icon to Save.


STEP 3  -  Deploy it as a Web App
---------------------------------
1. Click the blue  Deploy  button (top right)  >  New deployment.
2. Click the gear icon next to "Select type" and choose  Web app.
3. Set the fields:
      Description:   Jade Benefits forms
      Execute as:    Me (your email)
      Who has access:  Anyone
   (Anyone is required so your website visitors can submit. They only
    write data, they cannot read your sheet.)
4. Click  Deploy.
5. Click  Authorize access  and approve the permissions for your
   own Google account. If Google shows an "unverified app" warning,
   click "Advanced" then "Go to (project name)" and Allow. This is
   normal for your own scripts.
6. Copy the  Web app URL. It looks like:
      https://script.google.com/macros/s/AKfy...long.../exec


STEP 4  -  Paste the URL into the website
-----------------------------------------
1. Open the file  assets/js/sheet.js  in your project.
2. On this line:
      window.JADE_SHEET_ENDPOINT = "";
   paste your URL between the quotes, so it reads:
      window.JADE_SHEET_ENDPOINT = "https://script.google.com/macros/s/AKfy.../exec";
3. Save the file, then re-deploy your site (push to GitHub / Vercel).


DONE
----
Submit a test quote and a test contact message on your live site.
Within a second or two you will see new rows appear in your sheet,
on a "Quotes" tab and a "Contacts" tab.


UPDATING THE SCRIPT LATER
-------------------------
If you ever change Code.gs, you must Deploy again:
   Deploy  >  Manage deployments  >  pencil/edit  >  Version: New version  >  Deploy.
Otherwise the old version keeps running.


EMAIL ALERTS (already built in)
-------------------------------
The script also emails you at  Chloe@jadebenefits.com  every time a new
quote or contact comes in, so you do not have to watch the sheet.

Notes:
- During Step 3 authorization, Google will also ask to allow sending
  email on your behalf. Approve it. That is what powers the alerts.
- The first alert may land in your Spam/Promotions folder once. Mark it
  "Not spam" so future ones go to your inbox.
- To send alerts to a different address, open Code.gs and change the
  line near the top:  var NOTIFY_EMAIL = "Chloe@jadebenefits.com";
- Google allows a large number of these emails per day on a free
  account, far more than a small business site will ever need.
